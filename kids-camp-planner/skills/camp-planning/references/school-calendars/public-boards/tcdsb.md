### Summer Window (for camp planning)
- **Last school day**: June 25, 2026 (elementary) / June 26, 2026 (last PA day)
- **First fall day**: Estimated day after Labour Day 2026 (September 8, 2026; Labour Day is Sep 7)
- **Summer coverage needed**: ~52 weekdays (~11 weeks)

### Notes
- TCDSB calendar aligns with TDSB to minimize shared transportation costs
- Elementary PA day purposes: 2 parent-teacher conferences, 2 assessment/reporting, 3 provincial priorities (including half-day faith development)
- Secondary PA day purposes: 3 provincial priorities, 1 faith development, 3 system priorities/local goals
- Secondary schools may adjust a PA day to follow parent-teacher interview nights (via LSSAC)
