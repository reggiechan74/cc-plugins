## Financial Assistance & Subsidies

### Ontario Child Care Fee Subsidy
- Available through municipal children's services departments
- Income-tested; covers partial or full camp fees for eligible families
- Apply through local municipality (not provincial)
- Processing times vary: apply 4-8 weeks before camp starts

### Canada-Wide Early Learning and Child Care (CWELCC)
- Federal program reducing fees for licensed child care
- Some camp programs qualify if they are licensed child care
- Check with individual providers about CWELCC eligibility

### Canadian Tax Benefits
- **Child Care Expense Deduction (Line 21400)**: Camp fees are deductible as child care expenses
  - Maximum deduction per child varies by age
  - Under 7: up to $8,000/year
  - Ages 7-16: up to $5,000/year
  - Must have receipts; camp must provide tax receipts
- **Ontario Child Care Tax Credit**: Provincial credit on top of federal deduction
- Keep all camp receipts for tax filing

### Employer Benefits
- Some employers offer dependent care flexible spending accounts
- Check if employer provides child care subsidies or backup care programs

## Camp Types in Ontario

### By Structure
- **Traditional day camp**: Multi-activity, full-day, weekly registration
- **Specialty camp**: Focused on one area (STEM, arts, sports, coding, cooking)
- **Sports camp**: Single sport or multi-sport, often run by sports clubs
- **Academic camp**: Tutoring, enrichment, test prep (less common for younger ages)
- **Nature/outdoor camp**: Conservation areas, farms, forest schools

### By Provider Type
- **Municipal**: City-run, most affordable, HIGH FIVE registered
- **YMCA/YWCA**: Well-established, sliding scale fees, inclusive programs
- **Private**: Wide range of quality and cost, may offer specialized programming
- **University/College**: Campus-based programs, often STEM or arts focused
- **Non-profit**: Scouts, Guides, community organizations, cultural groups

### By Duration
- **Full-day**: Typically 9am-4pm
- **Half-day**: Morning or afternoon only (common for ages 4-5)
- **Extended day**: Before-care (7:00-8:30am) and/or after-care (4:00-6:00pm)

## Typical Cost Ranges (Ontario, approximate)

| Camp Type | Cost/Week | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Municipal day camp | $150-250 | Most affordable option |
| YMCA day camp | $200-300 | Sliding scale available |
| Private general day camp | $250-450 | Varies widely |
| Specialty camp (STEM, arts) | $300-500 | Materials often included |
| Sports camp (single sport) | $250-400 | Equipment may be extra |
| Premium/elite sport camp | $400-700+ | Coaching quality premium |
| Academic/tutoring camp | $300-500 | Smaller group sizes |

**Cost modifiers:**
- Sibling discounts: 5-15% off second child
- Early bird: 5-10% off if registered by deadline
- Multi-week: Some offer 5-10% for booking 3+ weeks
- Before/after care: $25-75/week additional
- Lunch program: $25-50/week if not included
