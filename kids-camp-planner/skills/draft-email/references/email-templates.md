# Email Templates

## Registration Email

```
Subject: Registration for [Child Name] - [Program Name], [Dates]

Hi [Camp Name] team,

I'd like to register [Child Name] (DOB: [date], Grade [X]) for the
[Program Name] program for the following week(s):
- [Week 1 dates]
- [Week 2 dates]

[If applicable: I'd also like to register [Child 2 Name] (DOB: [date],
Grade [X]) for [Program Name] for the same dates.]

We would also like to add:
- [Before care / After care / Both]
- [Lunch program, if applicable]

Please let me know the next steps for registration and payment.

[If applicable: Are there any early-bird or sibling discounts available?]

Thank you,
[Parent Name]
```

## Waitlist Follow-Up

```
Subject: Waitlist status - [Child Name] for [Program Name], [Dates]

Hi [Camp Name] team,

I'm following up on our waitlist registration for [Child Name] in the
[Program Name] program for the week(s) of [dates].

We placed our name on the waitlist on [date if known]. Could you let me
know our current status and the likelihood of a spot opening up?

[If applicable: We're also exploring backup options, so any update would
help with our planning. If a spot opens on short notice, we can confirm
within 24 hours.]

Thank you,
[Parent Name]
[Phone number]
```

## Special Needs / Dietary Email

```
Subject: Medical/dietary information for [Child Name] - [Program Name]

Hi [Camp Name] team,

[Child Name] is registered for [Program Name] during [dates]. I wanted to
share some important health information:

[Select relevant sections:]

**Allergies:**
- [Allergy 1]: [Severity and reaction type]
- [Allergy 2]: [Severity and reaction type]
- EpiPen will be provided: [Yes/No]
- Action plan attached: [Yes/No]

**Dietary restrictions:**
- [Restriction]: [Details]

**Medical conditions:**
- [Condition]: [Management plan]
- Medication required during camp: [Yes/No - details]

**Accommodations needed:**
- [Accommodation]: [Details]

I'd be happy to discuss these needs further or provide documentation from
our physician. Please let me know if you have any questions or if there's
a specific health form to complete.

Thank you for your attention to this,
[Parent Name]
[Phone number]
```

## Cancellation Email

```
Subject: Cancellation request - [Child Name], [Program Name], [Dates]

Hi [Camp Name] team,

I need to cancel [Child Name]'s registration for [Program Name] during the
week(s) of [dates].

[Optional: Brief reason, e.g., "Due to a change in our family schedule" -
no need for detailed explanation]

Per your cancellation policy [reference if known], could you please confirm:
- The cancellation has been processed
- Any applicable refund amount and timeline
- [If applicable: Whether the deposit can be applied to a different week]

Thank you for your understanding,
[Parent Name]
```

## Schedule / Logistics Inquiry Email

```
Subject: Logistics questions for [Child Name] - [Program Name], [Dates]

Hi [Camp Name] team,

[Child Name] is registered for [Program Name] during [dates]. I have a few
logistics questions before camp starts:

1. What are the exact dropoff and pickup locations and procedures?
2. [If applicable: Can someone other than the registered parent do pickup?
   What ID/authorization is needed?]
3. What should [Child Name] bring each day?
4. [If applicable: Is there a specific sunscreen/bug spray policy?]
5. [If applicable: How will we receive updates or communication during the
   camp day?]

Thank you,
[Parent Name]
[Phone number]
```
